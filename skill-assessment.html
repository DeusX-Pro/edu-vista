Professional Skill Assessment

For professionals looking to migrate to Australia or Canada, a positive Skill Assessment is the mandatory first step toward Permanent Residency. At Eduvista Solutions Limited, we specialize in helping Nigerian professionals navigate the rigorous requirements of assessing bodies such as ACS, Engineers Australia, VETASSESS, and WES for Canada. We ensure your qualifications and work experience are presented accurately to meet international standards.

  • ANZSCO/NOC Mapping
  • Document Legalization
  • Verified PR Pathways

Our Asaba-based consultants provide a thorough review of your career history to ensure your assessment is successful the first time.

Check Eligibility

A successful skill assessment determines your eligibility for points-tested visas. We assist with the technicalities of writing Career Episode Reports (CDR), RPL applications for ICT professionals, and coordinating with the relevant authorities to ensure your Nigerian work experience is fully recognized abroad.

The process typically begins with a detailed eligibility check where we review your degree and years of post-qualification experience. Whether you are an engineer, IT specialist, or healthcare professional, our guidance minimizes the risk of rejection.
